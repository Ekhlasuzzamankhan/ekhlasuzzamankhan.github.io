My passion

Sports have always been a significant part of my life — not just as a spectator, but as an active participant. I genuinely believe that the values instilled through sports translate directly into real-world success. Whether it is the patience required in a Test match or the split-second decision-making in football, every game teaches something meaningful.

I enjoy both playing and following sports at a competitive level. Being physically active keeps me grounded, focused, and energised — qualities that I carry into my academic and professional life as well.
