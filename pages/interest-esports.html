My perspective

I have been passionate about gaming across multiple platforms — PC, mobile, and consoles. What draws me to esports is not just the gameplay itself, but the remarkable ecosystem that has developed around it: professional leagues, international tournaments, dedicated fan communities, and a rapidly expanding industry worth billions globally.

As someone studying Electrical and Electronics Engineering, I also see esports through a technical lens — the hardware innovations, networking infrastructure, and software engineering that power competitive gaming are genuinely fascinating. I firmly believe esports has an extraordinary future, and its mainstream recognition is only just beginning.
